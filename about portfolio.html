About Karen Khalifah:

I am most passionate about the experiences we create as humans through multiple forms of interaction. Those moments that stay with you. I want to create those moments. I love the creative process, especially brainstorming and collaborating. I am an artist first and foremost so I approach any design problem through the eyes of a fine artist. I like to have a hands on approach using all five senses to gather information about potential clients and understanding their brand.

I feel most proud when a client is blown away by a project I have had the privilege to work on. The impact of what you do directly affecting the business, and the consumer.

My dream job is a place where creativity rules and brilliant ideas are being born constantly. An environment filled with people working smarter and not harder. A place where the sky is the limit and employees are constantly challenged to succeed, thrive, and excel.
